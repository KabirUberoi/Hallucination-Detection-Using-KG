Offensively, she made consistent contact, knew when to take the extra base, and excelled as a basestealer.

The NGBL was organized in Chicago to keep the best local players from migrating to the AAGPBL, which had been stabilized in 1943. After that, both circuits competed against each other to see who could associate itself with the biggest stars.
